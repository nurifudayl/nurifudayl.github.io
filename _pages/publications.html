Book

## Selected Works and Research Outputs
Research Area
Legitimacy and Political Authority
My work examines how political authority is justified, contested, and made intelligible through historically situated political languages.
Research Area
Modern Turkish Political Life and Constitutional Thought
My current research turns toward the 1960 constitution-making process in Türkiye, with particular attention to the intellectual worlds of jurists and the transformation of legal doctrines over time.
Project
Political Science Education, Games, Simulations, and AI
I am involved in projects that develop innovative practices in political science education through games, simulations, game design, emerging technologies, and artificial intelligence.
